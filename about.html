Who We Are

Stock Operator is a funded trading program that empowers traders worldwide by providing access to capital. Our mission is to discover, support, and grow talented traders by offering the resources they need to succeed.

How It Works

  • Choose a funding plan that fits your trading goals.
  • Pass our evaluation phase to demonstrate your skills.
  • Receive funding and trade with our capital.
  • Keep the majority of profits while we take care of the rest.

Why Choose Us

We believe in creating opportunities for traders of all levels. With transparent rules, fair profit splits, and support from industry professionals, Stock Operator helps you focus on trading while we provide the capital.
